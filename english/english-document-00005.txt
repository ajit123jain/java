<title>What is the Maastricht Discipline?  </title>
<date>20-3-2010</date>
<content> The term refers to conditions that all countries of the  European Union had to adhere to while adopting the Euro as a common currency. To do so, the countries had to show that their economic condition was stable and their fiscal deficit did not exceed 3%. Initially,  Poland and some other countries did not join because of their poor growth rate.   — Bhanu Pratap, Lucknow </content>
